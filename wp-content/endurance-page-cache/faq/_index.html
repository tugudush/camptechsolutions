FAQ

March 21, 2017 shaira

Below are the top 5 questions that we usually come across with. Please take the time to read it so you will be guided on how our process works. The full details will be discussed with you when you contact us.  We greatly value engagement and interaction with our prospects and clients thus, we want to make sure we talk to you. We are not a fan of automated responses.

1. I DON’T SEE ANY SIGN-UP BUTTON OR FORM ON THE SITE. WHERE CAN I SIGN UP?

Please go through our How it Works page. Our process is created in such a way that the sign-up button or page will not be directly accessed publicly. Why? We don’t want to take your money unless we are certain we can help you. We have seen so many businesses take customers money before engaging with them. When you send us an email, we will respond to you directly and will set up a call with you (Skype, WhatsApp, etc). This is where you will get access to the sign-up button or page.

2. HOW LONG WILL THIS PROCESS TAKE?

For in-house projects, we have to determine the time frame based on the discussion. We cannot give you the time frame unless we don’t know what it is that you exactly need and want. As for recruitment, the process usually takes 7 days for basic roles. If you have more complex requirements then it would take longer than that but a representative will be in touch with you to give you updates.

3. HOW MUCH DOES THE SERVICE COST?

The in-house project’s costs will be determined after discussing the things you want to be done. As for recruitment, we have a set fee which we will send once you have contacted us.

4. DO YOU HAVE ANY REFUND IF IN CASE I WILL NOT BE HAPPY WITH THE SERVICE?

Our refund system is very specific but aims at a win-win situation for us and our clients. For in-house projects, refund system is much more detailed as there are many factors involved.  As for recruitment, we will provide you with a 50% refund of the fee within 30 days. Please take note that within 30 days, our representative will be in touch with you to ensure that you are happy with your VA.

5. WHAT IS THE BEST WAY TO CONTACT YOU?

Since we work with people from different time zones, the best way to contact us would be through e-mail. We will respond to you within 24 hours on weekdays or business days. We don’t work on weekends but if you have urgent concerns, feel free to call the number listed below.
