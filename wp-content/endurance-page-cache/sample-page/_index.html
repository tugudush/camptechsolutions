How it Works

June 9, 2016 tugudush

We have listed the steps below on how our services work. This will guide you throughout the process.

STEP 1

Determine what you need. 

A. Do you need help with a project?

B. Do you need help with recruitment?

Once you have determined whether it’s A or B, proceed to the next step.

STEP 2

Send an email to support@camptechsolutions.com. 

Please make your details as detailed as possible. This will help us find out how we can best help you.

These are the relevant information that we need from you.

A. If you need help with a certain project, provide us with the following:

i. Type of project – e.g. website

ii.  Specific details about the project – e.g. e-commerce website, basic tabs only, images and content provided, etc.

iii. Time frame for the project – e.g. 1 month

iv. Budget for the project

v. All other information which you think are relevant to the project

B. If you need help with recruitment, provide us with the following:

i. Role that you need to fill – e.g. Bookkeeper

ii. Is the role project based or for long term employment?

iii. Responsibilities and requirements for the role – e.g. track sales, bookkeeping experience of 2 years, knows how       to use Xero, etc.

iv. Proposed rate  for the role

v. Work schedule for the role, tools used, and other important details

STEP 3

After we review the information that you sent us, we will respond to you within the next 24 hrs during business days. We don’t work on weekends to promote family and social time. For urgent matters, please give us a call at the number listed on our contact page.

We will request for a scheduled call to discuss in full details the responses to your requests. A representative is assigned to every client.

STEP 4

Once the things are clearly discussed on the call, we will prepare a contract which will be electronically signed. Aside from the contract, you will also receive a Camptech Project/ Recruitment Guide. This will serve as your starter guide.

STEP 5

Once the contract has been signed,  the real work starts. The time frame for the project will depend on what’s been agreed on the call by both parties. As for recruitment, it usually takes 7 days except for more complicated and in-demand roles. Another date for a call will be set for the delivery of the project or line-up of candidates for the role.

STEP 6

Assessment is usually done after 2 weeks since the project has been delivered or a candidate has been hired.
